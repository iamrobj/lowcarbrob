On starting my recent journey&nbsp;into intermittent fasting I&#8217;ve found that it&#8217;s actually not as difficult as I thought it&#8217;d be not to eat ALL the time. &nbsp;In fact I&#8217;ve found I actually don&#8217;t need everything I was eating previously, two main meals a day along with the occasional homemade indulgences in between (if I couldn&#8217;t still eat the good stuff I wouldn&#8217;t be doing it :p) and I&#8217;m good to go.

However before I took this dive into health and nutrition my go to drink at any coffee shop was a Mocha. &nbsp;Which, of course, is essentially caffeinated hot chocolate.. Mmmm. &nbsp;Now I obviously refuse to order that now, what with it being over 300+ empty calories&nbsp;and enough&nbsp;sugar to create a tsunami in my blood sugar levels, so I thought I&#8217;d try to find a way to make one&nbsp;that&#8217;s low carb while still tasting gooood &#8211; and this is the result. &nbsp;(It&#8217;s also got a decent amount of fat in it as I&#8217;m working on getting into ketosis atm)

Now bare in mind this is deliberately labelled take 1 because I know for sure with a little more experimentation I&#8217;ll eventually stumble upon the &#8220;Ultimate Low Carb Keto Friendly Dark Chocolate Mochachino&#8221; but for now, this is the winner! 🙂

Enjoy!
