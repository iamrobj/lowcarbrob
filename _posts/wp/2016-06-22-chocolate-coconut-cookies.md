At this moment in time I&#8217;m experimenting with a high fat, low carb diet &#8211; in fact, it&#8217;s pretty much a Ketogenic diet at this point. &nbsp;One thing I really miss though is snacks. &nbsp;I know we should only eat when we feel hungry and that food is the fuel our bodies need to run but my struggle recently has been&nbsp;this.. I like food. &nbsp;I like eating it, I like making it & I like sharing it with others.

So while I am only eating what I actually feel I need and not just because I&#8217;m bored or because it&#8217;s the socially acceptable thing to do (aka lunch at 12) I wanted to have something to hand I could indulge in a little when I felt like it. &nbsp;And soo&#8230; Cookies!!!!

Now this recipe is one I&#8217;ve been toying with for many weeks and has gone through many incarnations. &nbsp;I wanted it to be low carb (obviously) with a decent fat content and preferably low in protein so it doesn&#8217;t affect my macros. &nbsp;(Tip: If you want higher protein cookies swap out the double/heavy cream for Greek Yoghurt ðŸ˜‰ ) &nbsp;After burning, undercooking and making absolutely horrible cookies I got to this one! So far everyone I&#8217;ve&nbsp;had try one&nbsp;who are not into healthy eating&nbsp;have said it tastes like chocolate (woohoo!) and it&#8217;s kind&#8217;ve like cake (bonus!).
