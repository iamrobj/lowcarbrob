When I first started looking for low carb, healthy dessert like snacks I was very skeptical of &#8220;Fat Bombs&#8221;. &nbsp;Isn&#8217;t it unhealthy? &nbsp;Why would I need an explosion of fat? &nbsp;Does it even taste good? &nbsp;A couple months later and here I am posting my first &#8220;Fat Bomb&#8221; recipe.. Needless to say they&#8217;re actually not unhealthy and they can taste damn goood ðŸ™‚

One thing I noticed though from a lot of the great recipes online is that almost all of them involve either nut butter or coconut but&nbsp;none that I&#8217;ve found involve Avocado. &nbsp;Now with Avocado being the superfood that it is, and loaded with healthy fats, I figured I&#8217;d take a stab at an Avocado fat bomb. &nbsp;After a bit of trial and error I came up with this and omg does it taste good!! &nbsp;All my dessert like recipes I&#8217;ve made so far resemble cupcakes as that&#8217;s the only baking moulds&nbsp;I have, but in this case it actually makes this recipe much more fun to make and the result is awesome.

I wanted to keep the filling green for aesthetic reasons (isn&#8217;t it said our eyes eat first?) but as we all know Avocado definitely doesn&#8217;t have a dessert like taste or texture. &nbsp;Enter the Peppermint. &nbsp;One teaspoon of that and the&nbsp;green filling with that taste suddenly makes so much sense. &nbsp;I also stumbled upon a trick to make actual &#8220;cups&#8221; (think peanut butter cups) from the mixture so it&#8217;s an actual cup(cake) with a filling and not just a mess of frozen ingredients.

Anyways, enough rambling from me on how and why I came up with this. &nbsp;Instead, try them.. &nbsp;They take 15 mins to make.. Then let me know what you think ðŸ˜€ &nbsp;Enjoy!
