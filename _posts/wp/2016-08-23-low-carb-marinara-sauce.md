This recipe was completely a DIY job that ended up becoming a regular meal prep item for me because it tasted so good! &nbsp;I use it to make chilli (mix it in with some heavy cream or coconut milk&nbsp;ðŸ‘Œ) and it also makes for a great pizza sauce (for those low carb cauliflower pizza days, or my latest discovery &#8211; Meatza!! &nbsp;That recipe will be up next week ðŸ˜‰ ).

Essentially it was the usual criteria &#8211; low carb, gluten free, sugar free and in this case I also wanted to stay away from tomato puree as I haven&#8217;t come across one I like the ingredients of yet. &nbsp;Plus tomatoes are carby enough (relatively speaking) without using a concentrate form of them. &nbsp;And so I came up with this, a single batch is only 6g carbs &#8211; try and find a pizza sauce that can beat that! ðŸ˜€

Anyways, I&#8217;m rambling, sauce is sauce what else can I say except.. Enjoy!
