This one got a lot of positive feedback on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/lowcarbrob/) so I figured I&#8217;d better rearrange my recipe posting schedule & make this my next one! &nbsp;I haven&#8217;t really had much of a craving for chocolate bars since going low carb however as I was going through my Feedly feed I saw a recipe which sent my brain into a spiral of taking a stab at making one &#8211; and it turned out pretty damn good! &nbsp;Then I realised if I kept these in the freezer, instead of the fridge, they essentially become choc ice! &nbsp;Even better!!<figure id="attachment_235" class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 1034px">

<img class="wp-image-235 size-full" src="http://www.lowcarbrob.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/IMG_20160811_153826.jpg" alt="Almond Butter variation" width="1024" height="1024" srcset="http://www.lowcarbrob.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/IMG_20160811_153826.jpg 1024w, http://www.lowcarbrob.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/IMG_20160811_153826-150x150.jpg 150w, http://www.lowcarbrob.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/IMG_20160811_153826-300x300.jpg 300w, http://www.lowcarbrob.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/IMG_20160811_153826-768x768.jpg 768w, http://www.lowcarbrob.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/IMG_20160811_153826-600x600.jpg 600w" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" /><figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">Almond Butter variation</figcaption></figure> 

Incidentally the recipe I stumbled across which got my brain juices flowing was one for&nbsp;[Bounty Ice Cream](http://ketodietapp.com/Blog/post/2016/08/10/keto-bounty-ice-cream-with-magic-shell) over on [KetoDietApp](http://ketodietapp.com/Blog/) (if you haven&#8217;t yet definitely check out Martina&#8217;s site &#8211; it&#8217;s&nbsp;a great lowcarb/keto resource). &nbsp;Now of course this is nothing like a chocolate bar but&nbsp;the word &#8220;Bounty&#8221; triggered an avalanche of thoughts that led me to concocting this delicious fat bomb! &nbsp;It&#8217;s mainly avocado for&nbsp;all of it&#8217;s health benefits alongside various forms of coconut for form and texture, topped with a dash of chocolate ðŸ™‚

If you choose to keep the bars in the freezer (like I do) you have some delicious choc ice fat bombs, however if you choose to keep them in the fridge you end up with some delicious hazelnut chocolate bars.. The choice is yours! &nbsp;(If you do keep them in the freezer I&#8217;d take them out 10 mins before you&#8217;re gonna eat them so they&#8217;re not rock hard ðŸ˜‰ )

Enjoy!
