Cheesecake. &nbsp;My speciality, or at least it used to be. &nbsp;Every dinner I went to I&#8217;d take a cheesecake and it&#8217;d always go down a treat. &nbsp;That was, of course, before I ditched gluten, sugar and everything else that goes with it for a much healthier lifestyle. &nbsp;Since then I&#8217;ve been experimenting with all kinds ways to reproduce my signature dishes, substituting the not so healthy ingredients for healthier alternatives and this has to be the best result so far!

I actually took it to a dinner recently, and without anyone knowing what was in it the feedback I got was amazing! &nbsp;It goes without saying the shop bought cake that was also on the table was not touched by any of the guests&nbsp;üòÅ

Then there was the social media response which was incredible and that was just going off a picture! &nbsp;So, due to popular demand, here is the recipe for my low carb, gluten free, sugar free, keto and paleo friendly.. Cheesecake. &nbsp;Enjoy!
